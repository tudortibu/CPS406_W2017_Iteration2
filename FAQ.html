  1. How do I check the status of a report?

  Click on the reports tab at the top of the page.

  2. How do I change my personal information?

  Click on the profile tab at the top of the page and enter the information you wish to

  change. Next, click on the save button.

  3. How do I contact an official?

  Click on the contact us tab at the top of the page to see the contact information of the officials

  4. How long does it usually take for an official to respond to a report?

  This will depend on the nature of the report and it's location. High priority reports such as power or

  water outages will take about one to two days to respond to the report. Medium priority reports such as

  potholes or mold will take about one to two weeks for a response. Low priority reports such as teenagers

  loitering on your lawn will take three to four weeks for a response.

  5. Raccoons are raiding all the garbage bins in our neighborhood, what should I do?

  Click on the reports tab at the top of the page and create a report
